We Shape Digital Experiences

Nexus is a creative digital studio founded in 2015 with a mission to build meaningful connections between brands and their audiences through innovative design and technology.

Our Journey

2015

Founded in a Garage

What started as two friends with laptops in a small garage has grown into an award-winning digital studio. Our first project was a local bakery website that still stands today.

2017

First Major Client

We landed our first Fortune 500 client, proving that small studios could deliver enterprise-level quality with a personal touch they couldn't find elsewhere.

2019

Expanded to 15 Team Members

As demand grew, we carefully expanded our team with top talent from around the world, each bringing unique perspectives to our creative process.

2021

Opened New York Office

Our physical presence grew with a beautiful workspace in Manhattan, designed to inspire creativity and collaboration for our team and clients.

2023

250+ Projects Delivered

We reached a major milestone of serving over 200 clients across 15 countries, with projects ranging from startups to global brands.
